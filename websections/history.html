Bethel's History

2002:Bethel began as a group of students who praised God every Friday through songs. Later, they transformed into a worship team dedicated to praise and worship

2003: Bethel appointed its first leader, named MUSAFIRI Jean de Dieu. That same year, Bethel was asked to become a Chorale, but it declined, preferring to remainGroupe de Louange et Adoration.

2004: President MUSAFIRI did not return to school, so Jean Paul, then a second-year student, became president.

2005: Bethel faced challenges, including being prohibited from singing and lacking representation in the church committee. On May 17, 2005, it was officially allowed to become a Chorale by both the school administration and the church, and was named Bethel Choir . In 2016, the name was extended to include Family

2008:Graduated members decided that their divine calling did not end at G.S.O.B, and they launched a ministry called Bethel Ministry. That year, they also established Bethel’s mission: "Binyuze muritwe Imana yigaragarize abandi".

2012: The Bethel Choir logo was introduced. Bethel celebrated its first anniversary, marking seven years (7-Year B-Anniversary). That same year, a sports division was launched within Bethel, called B-Sport.

2013: A football competition named B-CHAMPION.was initiated.

2015:New uniforms were purchased for the choir.

2016: A week of thanksgiving was organized to celebrate Bethel’s journey.

2018-2019: We achieved many things but among them we had time to teach members more about having you personal prayer with God,reading the word of God. We had 2 songs that we recorded. We had many sorties out side of the college. ( foursquare Gospel church)

2023-2024: We recorded a new audio song titled DUFITIMANIKORA.

2024-2025: We organized a fellowship meal for the choir, and we are currently working on creating a website for the choir. In addition, we spread the message in every possible way, which helped many young people grow spiritually and gain a deeper understanding of the Word of God.

2017 - 2018 and 2019-2023 : these historical chapters are still being completed. We’ll continue updating this information soon!

Responsibilities & Duties of B- Committe

  • President: Oversees all choir operations, coordinates meetings, sets vision, and represents the choir in official matters.

  • Vice President: BSupports the president, steps in when the president is unavailable, and helps manage internal coordination.

  • Secretary: Handles documentation, records meeting minutes, manages schedules, and communicates official updates.

  • Advisor: Offers strategic counsel, helps with long-term planning, and mentors the leadership team.

  • Mère (Mother): Provides emotional and spiritual support, mentors younger members, and fosters unity and care within the group.

  • Père (Father): Offers guidance and discipline, ensures moral integrity, and supports the choir’s spiritual growth.

  • Song Leader: Directs musical rehearsals, selects songs, leads performances, and ensures vocal harmony and excellence.

  • Intercessor: Leads prayer sessions, organizes spiritual retreats, and ensures the choir remains grounded in faith

  • Disciplinary: Maintains order, enforces choir rules, handles conflicts, and promotes respectful behavior.

  • IT: Manages technical needs like sound systems, digital archives, presentations, and online presence.
