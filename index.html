A word from El Presidente

El Presidente KWIZERA Clever

Welcome to the Bethel Choir family, a ministry rooted in worship and service to our Lord Jesus Christ. As the Psalmist declares, “Sing to the Lord a new song; sing to the Lord, all the earth” (Psalm 96:1), we are committed to lifting our voices in praise, spreading the gospel through music, and nurturing spiritual growth among our members and community. Guided by Colossians 3:16, which reminds us to let the message of Christ dwell richly among us through psalms, hymns, and songs from the Spirit, our mission is to glorify God, inspire hearts, and build a fellowship where faith and music unite. May this platform be a place of encouragement, joy, and a reminder that “Let everything that has breath praise the Lord” (Psalm 150:6).

Mission

To glorify God through the art of singing and music, spreading the message of salvation, and encouraging believers in their faith through powerful and heartfelt performances.

Vision

To be a leading voice in Christian worship across Rwanda and beyond, known for excellence, unity, and deep spiritual impact, fostering a generation of worshippers.
