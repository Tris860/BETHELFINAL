What is Bethel Family Choir?

Bethel Family Choir is a dynamic Christian choir founded in 2005 at Groupe Scolaire Officiel de Butare (Indatwa n' Inkesha School) in Huye, Rwanda. We are composed of youth students from GSOB. Our spiritual home is the Groupe Protestant church, where our activities primarily focus on passionate **praise and worship**.

To explore our founding and journey, view our full History.

Our Core Values

Truth

Our commitment to truth is unwavering, rooted in the Gospel. It guides our conduct, music, and relationships, ensuring integrity in all we do.

Love

We foster a family environment where Christ's love is lived out, extending unconditional support and acceptance to every member and the wider community.

Unity

Unity is the backbone of our harmony, ensuring that all voices and efforts are aligned toward the common goal of glorious worship and service.

Bethel logo

Unity, Truth, and Love: epresent the pillars on which Bethel is founded.

The Vine Cluster : Once someone arrives at Bethel, they are expected to share the Good News and bear fruit among their peers, as our message says: BICIYE MURI TWE IMANA YIGARAGARIZE ABANDI

The Lampstand : ased on the Gospel as written in Matthew 5:14 :"You are the light of the world" Bethel chose to include this in our logo to remind us that we must shine light upon the world.

The Gate in the Clouds: As previously explained, the Bible tells us that Bethel is the gate of heaven. We desired to be a gateway that leads to heaven, in line with our mission.

The Bible : The Bible represents the message we sing about — a message drawn directly from the Word of God.

Light from the Cross: This symbolizes that God reveals Himself through us, and that we too have a source like “Bethel” the light we received when Jesus gave Himself for us

Musical Notes: After doing all else, we must never forget the calling to worship and glorify God through music.

The Circle (Fence): TWe chose to encircle Bethel’s core values with a unified ring to represent the unity and love within Bethel as one family. "Bethel niferme".

Core Departments

Bethel Army (B-Army)

B-Army: The discipline and mobilization arm, responsible for internal order and external mission outreach.

Lounge Ministries

Lounge Ministries: Dedicated to fostering fellowship, mentorship, and the spiritual welfare of members through small groups.

Signature Events

Our annual activities and powerful events:

  • B-Mounts: An intensive, annual spiritual retreat focused on deep prayer, fasting, and spiritual revival.
  • B-Generations: A powerful gathering connecting current members with Bethel alumni for networking, wisdom, and continuity.
  • B-Sports: Organized sporting activities designed to promote teamwork, healthy bodies, and friendly competition among members.
  • B-Bye Bye: A celebrated send-off event honoring graduating members, welcoming them into the alumni network.
  • B-Anniversary: A grand celebration marking the founding and continued legacy of the choir.

Bethel's Mission & Vision

Bethel's Mission

To glorify God through the art of singing and music, spreading the message of salvation, and encouraging believers in their faith through powerful and heartfelt performances.

Bethel's Vision

To be a leading voice in Christian worship across Rwanda and beyond, known for excellence, unity, and deep spiritual impact, fostering a generation of worshippers.
